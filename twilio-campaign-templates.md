# TextLoop - A2P Campaign Message Templates

## Campaign Information

**Campaign Name:** TextLoop Customer Follow-ups

**Use Case:** Customer Care / Account Notifications

**Campaign Description:**
Automated follow-up messages sent to customers who have requested quotes for contractor services. Messages remind customers about pending estimates and check in on their decision-making process.

**Business Purpose:**
To improve customer communication and reduce lost opportunities for contractors by ensuring timely follow-ups on quotes and estimates.

## Opt-In / Opt-Out Information

### Opt-In Method:
**Verbal consent at time of quote**

Customers provide their phone number when requesting a quote for contractor services. By providing their contact information, they consent to receive follow-up messages about their estimate.

### Opt-In Message (if required):
```
Thanks for your quote request! You'll receive text message updates about your estimate. Reply STOP to opt out anytime.
```

### Opt-Out Instructions:
Customers can reply with any of the following to stop receiving messages:
- STOP
- UNSUBSCRIBE
- CANCEL
- END
- QUIT

**Opt-Out Confirmation:**
```
You have been unsubscribed from TextLoop messages and will not receive further texts. Reply START to resubscribe.
```

### Help Message:
```
TextLoop: Automated follow-ups for your contractor estimate. Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Questions? Contact [Business Phone]
```

## Additional Campaign Details

### Message Frequency:
- Maximum 5 messages per customer over 12 days
- Messages sent during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM local time)
- No weekend messages

### Monthly Volume Estimate:
- Starting volume: 100-500 messages/month
- Growth projection: 500-2,000 messages/month

### Target Audience:
- Homeowners and business owners
- People who have requested contractor quotes
- US-based customers only

### Message Content:
- **Informational**: Estimate status updates
- **Reminder**: Follow-up on pending decisions
- **Professional**: Business-appropriate language
- **Non-promotional**: No sales or marketing content

## How to Use These Templates in Twilio

1. Go to **Messaging** → **Regulatory Compliance** → **US A2P 10DLC**
2. Click **Register a Campaign** (after registering your business)
3. Fill out the form:
   - **Campaign Use Case**: Select "Customer Care" or "Account Notifications"
   - **Sample Message #1**: Copy Message 1 example above
   - **Sample Message #2**: Copy Message 2 example above
   - **Sample Message #3**: Copy Message 3 example above (if asked for more)
   - **Opt-In Process**: "Verbal consent at time of quote"
   - **Opt-Out Process**: "Reply STOP to unsubscribe"
   - **Message Flow**: "Automated follow-up sequence over 12 days"

4. Submit for review

## Tips for Approval

✅ **Do:**
- Use clear, professional language
- Include opt-out instructions
- Explain the business purpose clearly
- Be honest about message frequency
- Include real examples with names/projects

❌ **Don't:**
- Use promotional language ("Buy now!", "Sale!")
- Promise instant approvals
- Exaggerate message volume
- Include URLs or links in samples (can trigger review flags)
- Use all caps or excessive punctuation
